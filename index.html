WHAT IS HALLOWEEN?

Halloween is the one of the oldest traditions. People dressed up to pretend to be something or someone else. Strangers giving out candy, still I have not seen someone pull out a trick. I dont think I would anyway; however Halloween is still a good way of self expression even if it is just for one day. The thought of being scary or scared is the thrill people look forward that celebrate the tradition. Its a rush that cannot be explained but only to experience. All walks of life enjoy it from kids to the elderly. Although it doesnt always have to be scary its really about using your imagination.
